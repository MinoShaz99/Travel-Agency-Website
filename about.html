Welcome to Sri Lanka Views 2024–2025! Explore a land rich in tradition, culture, and hospitality. From ancient royal towns to breathtaking rainforests, mountain landscapes, and pristine beaches, Sri Lanka offers something for everyone.

For Cultural Enthusiasts and History Lovers:

  • Anuradhapura: One of the largest ancient monuments and a pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
  • Polonnaruwa: A stunning archaeological park with well-preserved ruins and beautiful landscapes.
  • Sigiriya: Home to the iconic Lion Rock fortress.
  • Ritigala: An ancient Buddhist monastery set in a unique microclimate.
  • Kandy: The last royal city, surrounded by tea plantations and rainforests.
  • Ambakke Devalaya and Enderagala Wana Senasuna: Marvel at intricate wood carvings and historic temples.
  • Cave Temple of Dambulla: The largest and best-preserved cave temple in Sri Lanka.

For Nature Lovers and Wildlife Enthusiasts:

  • Sinharaja Rainforest: A UNESCO World Heritage site teeming with diverse wildlife.
  • World's End in Horton Plains: A dramatic cliff offering stunning views.
  • Ella: A scenic village with photogenic bridges, tea plantations, and hiking opportunities.
  • Royal Botanical Garden of Peradeniya: Home to exotic plants and beautiful landscapes.
  • Nuwara Eliya: Known for its cool climate and tea plantations.
  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage: A sanctuary for orphaned and injured elephants.
  • Yala and Minneriya National Parks: See elephants, leopards, and a variety of bird species.

For Beach Lovers and Adventurers:

Sri Lanka’s beaches cater to every type of traveler. From kite surfing in Kalpitiya to swimming in the safe waters of the east coast, and surfing in Bentota and Hikkaduwa, there’s a perfect spot for everyone. Check local conditions and heed safety warnings, especially during monsoon seasons.

Classical Tour Programs:

  • 3-Day Tour: Pinnawala, Kandy, Sigiriya, Dambulla
  • 5-Day Tour: Pinnawala, Dambulla, Sigiriya, Anuradhapura or Polonnaruwa, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya
  • 8-Day Tour: Pinnawala, Dambulla, Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Ella, Yala
